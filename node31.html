Stress tensor

Since the stress tensor is symmetric and is composed by all real numbers, there exist 3 real-valued eigenvalues that we call principal stresses and denote . Each principal stress (eigenvalue) is associated with a principal direction (eigenvector). The stress tensor is written with principal stresses in the diagonal and zeros in the off-diagonal when we write the stress tensor in a coordinate system aligned with directions of the principal stresses. Usually, the principal stresses are ordered from top to bottom starting with at the top.
